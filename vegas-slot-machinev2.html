Frequently Asked Questions

When would I be able to see my token balance?
Qualified purchasers who purchase through Gate.io Startups will have their purchased tokens deposited to their Gate.io wallets immediately prior to the Launch of $VEGASCOIN on the Gate.io platform.
Is it possible for the citizens or residents of the US to participate in the Token Sale?
Per the Terms of Gate.io Startups, there are limitations on who may participate in the pre-launch sale. Anyone with a Gate.io account can acquire $VEGASCOIN once it has officially launched on Gate.io and through other trading platforms where it may be listed thereafter.
Is there a KYC process involved?
$VEGASCOIN has no KYC directly, as the token will only be made available through a third-party platform which has it’s own KYC in place. Please refer to the KYC requirements of the platform from which you are seeking to acquire $VEGASCOIN. It is NOT directly sold from the developers or from Vegascoins.io. Nor is it ever for sale or exchange by use of any fiat currency.
What will happen to the unsold tokens?
So long as the pre-launch sale is successful (proving that we have met our sale goal as disclosed on Gate.io Startups) It will be shortly thereafter listed on Gate.io for public trading. Prior to launch on Gate.io all unsold tokens from the Pre-launch Sale Supply of 600,000,000,000 $VEGASCOIN tokens will be placed into the Public Sale Token Supply and sold according to the terms of the Public Sale Token Supply in our white paper.
Which cryptocurrencies can I use to participate in the Token Sale?
This will be dictated by Gate.io, but it is anticipated that qualified purchasers may acquire $VEGASCOIN tokens in the pre-launch sale process using USDT.
Are there any restrictions that involve a minimum or a maximum transaction limit?
This will be dictated by Gate.io and purchase limitations are affected by both Gate.io’s formula for calculating the amount any one user can acquire; as well as by the limitation of 600,000,000,000 $VEGASCOIN tokens made available for the pre-launch sale. It is anticipated that we will see not less than 15,000 participants in the pre-launch sale process. At the pre-launch price of USDT 0.0001, each participant could acquire up to an average of $4,000 USDT or 40 Million $VEGASCOIN tokens.
Where is the company registered? Where is the Coinpool office located?
Vegascoin will be launched from Monaco.
