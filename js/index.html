About $VEGASCOIN

Why Is $VEGASCOIN Special?

Never HODL flat coins again.

$VEGASCOIN is an ERC-20 token created with the specific intent of providing day traders a coin whose sole function is to rise and fall in value regularly, offering maximum tradability. The fundamentals behind this token are intended to purposefully create movement and volatility in the value of the coin. This provides ample opportunity for crypto traders to consistently trade with a coin that has the capacity to avoid flatlining due to the nature of the project plan and future coin disbursement schedules. Our Slot Machine Themed Widget will purposefully promote value fluctuations through the random dictating of either injecting $VEGASCOIN into circulation via airdrops, removing existing supply via burning, and or minting new supply. This will be called a Tile Roll. The amount of any of these weekly Stimulus Events will also be determined by a randomized roulette-style wheel spin once weekly. This will be called a Number Drawing. These two events are cumulatively referred to as Stimulus Events.

The resulting value fluctuations will offer consistently prime trading opportunities. The Token Stimulus Events happen once weekly through the daily spin of the Slot-style widget. In keeping with the theme of a casino style slot machine, the widget will spin three reels. Each reel will have a series of Airdrop, Mint, and Burn icons. Every seven days, all spin results for each event represented by their respective icons will be tallied to determine which event was spun the most times during that weekly round. With the majority determined, the roulette wheel will be triggered to spin and randomly generate the resulting quantity of $VEGASCOIN tokens to be Minted, Burned or Airdropped for that weekly round. Once the weekly Stimulus Event has finished, and all $VEGASCOIN tokens have been disbursed accordingly, the next weekly round begins, repeating indefinitely. Vegascoins.io will host a $VEGASCOIN Stimulus Event countdown feed widget which can be downloaded and attached to your desktop to keep you updated on the daily outcome of each spin as the weekly rounds progress through each week.

How It Works

Using complex coding and mathematics, we're able to determine the best way to keep $VEGASCOIN moving. Take a look at how it breaks down:

Recurring Stimulus Events

A Stimulus Event is the combination of a Tile Roll & a Number Drawing. Tile Rolls happen once every 24 hours. Once 7 Tile Rolls have been completed, each tile will be tallied. The most frequently drawn tile will confirm which Stimulus Event reward will be given out. Once every 7 days, a Number Drawing decides how much of the reward is to be executed. Upon the completion of the Number Drawing, the current Stimulus Event will conclude, and the next one will repeat this process.

Stimulus Event Rewards

Airdrops, Minting, & Burning. These are the only possible results that a Stimulus Event can give. Which reward to be given will be determined via Tile Rolls. The amount of the reward to be given will be determined via Number Drawings.

Reserve Wallet

The Reserve Wallet is where the Stimulus Events take effect. Any Minted VegasCoin will be put into this wallet. If a Stimulus Event dictates a Burn, the Reserve Wallet is where the Burn will take place. Most importantly, Airdrops come from this wallet as well. More $VEGASCOIN Minted into the Reserve Wallet means a higher ratio of Airdrops, and vice versa with Burning.

Randomly Generated

The Vegas themed slot-style and roulette-style widgets are 100% randomly generated results. Once this features goes live, we will provide a link to publically view our code.

Purposefully Randomized

As previously mentioned, Stimulus Events are randomized. But why? We’ll tell you. All crypto traders know the hazards social media can have on a coin. Tweets, News Press, celebrities, or other public figures can have a powerful impact on crypto values. $VEGASCOIN attempts to hedge against that by enacting purposefully randomized Stimulus Events that cannot be affected by any public figure. This process is written firmly into our code and made part of the Smart Contract, ensuring these events remain truly randomized to fight against any unwanted manimupation.

Dev Secured

As with all crypto tokens, $VEGASCOIN is immutably created on the blockchain, and its rules and guidelines are set, with full and transparent views of all transactions that occur from the moment of token creation. Our site offers the highest levels of encryption and security to ensure the Token Stimulus Events remain securely randomized in perpetuity.
